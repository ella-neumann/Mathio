About Mathio

Teacher Capabilities

Teachers have the option to choose what kind of visualization they want to pair with an assigned question. When teachers select the kind of question that they want, they are taken to the next screen which allows the teacher to set the specifics of the problem that they are assigning to a student.

Interaction

When a teacher has created math content for their students, they can share a code with their class through email. The email will contain a code for students to enter into the Mathio Class Code page. The page is visually simple and allows for students to just focus on joining the classroom. Once the class code has been entered, the student will be brought to the home page of the math classroom. We can think about this feature as the hallway where students enter the classroom from.

Student Capabilities

Student users have access to visualizations that assist them in answering specific math questions, giving them a deeper understanding of vital fundamental math concepts while reducing barriers to learning. These depictions are designed to be simple, intuitive, and powerful.

Why Mathio?

About Mathio

Mathio understands that it is challenging to use tactile learning aids such as blocks, counting tools, and 3D shapes while learning online. We have created virtual learning tools that help teachers supplement the tactical learning lost online for 3rd and 4th grade math students. This application assists students in building skills that allow them to easily shift between practical and symbolic representations of mathematical concepts. Mathio is equipped with visualizations and intractables that relate directly to studentsâ€™ lived experiences. The application also serves as a formative assessment tool for teachers to gauge student understanding in key math curriculum areas. Teachers can see which students correctly answered assigned math questions and how long the students took to answer each question.

We want online learning to be engaging for all

Watch a video to learn more about Mathio

Our computing system allows students to have a more hands-on learning experience online. By offering students online learning support tools such as fraction visualization, shapes, clocks, and money, we are bringing the students one step closer to an in-person school learning experience. Giving students tactical learning tools will allow all students to have the opportunity to learn hands-on during online learning. This in turn will hopefully level the learning playing field for students with access to differing support networks at home.
